Mobility Data#

In this pilot study, we leverage mobility data to estimate trade activity (tentatively both formal and informal) and provide insights in the aftermath of the earthquakes near Nurdağı, Türkiye on February 6, 2023.

Data Availability Statement#

Data are available upon request through the Development Data Partnership. Licensing and access information for all other datasets are included in the documentation.

Limitations#

Caution

The mobility data represents a subset of the total population in an area, specifically only users that turned on the Location Services device setting on their mobile device. This is not the total population density.
